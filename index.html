About the app

Soccer 1 X 2 score prediction lets users predict football match scores, join private pools, and compare performance on leaderboards.

The app is designed for entertainment. It is not a betting or gambling app, and in-app purchases unlock app features only. They do not improve prediction outcomes and do not guarantee rewards.

Users can explore parts of the app as guests and sign in when they want to link progress to an account, join pools, or use account-based features.

Features

  • Predict exact football scores before kickoff.
  • Create or join private pools with friends.
  • Track standings, fixtures, and knockout draw progress.
  • Compare results in global and pool-specific leaderboards.
  • Restore purchases and manage feature access inside the app.
